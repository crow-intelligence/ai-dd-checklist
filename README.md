# AI DD Checklist

An open-source AI and NLP technical due diligence checklist, maintained by [Crow Intelligence](https://crowintelligence.org/).

> **Public beta.** This is a public beta release. The questions are a mix of our own — drawn from real engagements — items contributed via feedback, and unedited LLM suggestions we have not yet pruned. Expect rough edges; that is the point of a beta.

## The checklist

[AI_NLP_DD_CHECKLIST.md](./AI_NLP_DD_CHECKLIST.md) — 76 questions across ten sections, from "is the AI real?" through evaluation methodology, LLM-specific concerns, security, and EU AI Act readiness. Each question is tagged with a severity tier (T1 dealbreaker, T2 material, T3 maturity signal).

Short on time? Start with the **[Top 20 first-meeting triage](./AI_NLP_DD_CHECKLIST.md#top-20-first-meeting-triage)** and the **[stage-appropriate expectations table](./AI_NLP_DD_CHECKLIST.md#stage-appropriate-expectations)** in the preamble.

## Who uses it

We use this checklist during our own AI and NLP technical due diligence engagements at Crow Intelligence. It is published as a public artefact under CC BY 4.0 — use it, adapt it, take it into your own engagements.

## Contributing

Contributions are welcome:

- Open a pull request on this repository with the change, a brief reason, and a citation where applicable.
- Or email us at [hello@crowintelligence.org](mailto:hello@crowintelligence.org).

We are most interested in items the checklist gets wrong, items that have aged badly, and items that real diligence calls have shown to be missing.
